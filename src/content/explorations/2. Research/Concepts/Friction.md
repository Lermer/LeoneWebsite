# Summary 

Fiction as a effort required to move through something.

A idea that has hooked in my mind recently is the idea of designing intentional friction into work. For years the goal of design has been to make everything easier and more accessible. 

But why is effort necessarily a bad thing? Effort can create meaning, value and memory. 

Effort is a key factor in [[Transformation]]




The idea of positive effort has long existed in [[Game Design]] where creating changes for the player is part of the goal, why don't we see it in more
